## 📖 About

Bunjee.ai is a comprehensive AI-powered HR platform built for **HR teams and recruiters**. It transforms every stage of the talent lifecycle — from publishing a job post to onboarding new hires, running employee assessments, and enabling intelligent internal communications — using AI avatars and deep candidate analysis.

Most hiring processes make it hard to see the person behind the CV. Bunjee helps organisations understand potential with depth and confidence, replacing manual, time-consuming workflows with intelligent automation.

-----

## 🚀 Platform Modules

### 🎯 Module 1 — Hiring

An end-to-end AI hiring pipeline that handles everything from job creation to final candidate selection.

|Step|Action                                                                                                        |
|----|--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
|1   |Create a job post within the platform                                                                         |
|2   |Publish to multiple channels (LinkedIn, Indeed, and 50+ job boards) in one click                              |
|3   |Collect all incoming CVs in a centralised inbox                                                               |
|4   |AI analyses every CV and generates ranked long/short lists with scores and detailed reasoning                 |
|5   |Automatically send AI avatar interview invitation emails with a secure interview link                         |
|6   |Candidate clicks the link and completes an async video interview with an AI avatar                            |
|7   |Platform records the interview, generates a full transcript, performs analysis, and produces a detailed report|
|8   |HR receives a clear picture of the most promising candidates and proceeds to face-to-face interviews          |

-----

### 🚀 Module 2 — Onboarding

Digital onboarding delivered by a trained AI avatar — available on any device, at any time.

- **Generic avatars** available out of the box for instant deployment
- **Custom avatars** trained on your company’s brand, culture, tone, and documentation
- Covers company policies, culture, tools, team introductions, and compliance
- New hires can complete onboarding at their own pace before or on day one
- Real-time completion tracking and engagement dashboard for HR

-----

### 📊 Module 3 — Assessments

AI-powered employee assessments for performance cycles, pulse checks, and ad-hoc evaluations.

- Supports **bi-annual and custom-schedule** assessments
- Choice of **AI avatar conversation** (spoken, interactive) or **text-based** structured forms
- AI analyses all responses and identifies strengths, growth areas, and engagement signals
- Managers receive individual and team-level insight reports
- Consistent, objective, and scalable across all departments and seniority levels

-----

### 💬 Module 4 — Internal Communications

Train a custom AI avatar of your HR Manager or CEO to represent your company internally and externally.

- Avatar is trained on company documents, FAQs, announcements, and communication guidelines
- Employees can ask HR policy questions and get instant, accurate answers — 24/7
- CEO avatar can deliver personalised company-wide messages at scale, in the CEO’s voice and style
- Deploy via internal portal, Slack, Microsoft Teams, or a public-facing page
- Suitable for both **internal communications** (employees) and **external communications** (candidates, partners)

## 🚢 Deployment

This is a **static HTML site** — it can be deployed anywhere.

**Netlify (recommended):**

1. Go to [app.netlify.com/drop](https://app.netlify.com/drop)
1. Drag and drop `index.html`
1. Live URL generated instantly

**GitHub Pages:**

1. Go to repository **Settings → Pages**
1. Set source branch to `main`, folder to `/ (root)`
1. Save — your site will be live at `https://alpcapa.github.io/Bunjee`

**Vercel:**

1. Import repository at [vercel.com/new](https://vercel.com/new)
1. Set Framework Preset to **Other**
1. Deploy
